Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is ranked First in the Swacchatha Ranking in the Category " Technical Institutions" for Clean Campus initiative of MHRD. The Award was presented to the University' by Shri Prakash Javadekar, Hon'ble Minister of MHRD, Govt of India at a function in Ashoka Hotel, New Delhi on 14.9.2017. On behalf of Amrita University, Dr.S.Mahadevan, Deputy Dean, Amrita School of Engineering - Coimbatore received the award.
